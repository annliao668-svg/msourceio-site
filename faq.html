Buyer FAQ

Frequently asked questions from Pilates sock buyers

Clear answers to the questions buyers usually ask before sampling, pricing, production, and shipment.

What is your MOQ for custom Pilates socks?

MOQ depends on the product style, material choice, and customization scope. A practical MOQ can be discussed based on your project direction and order plan.

Can you add our logo to the socks?

Yes. The current messaging supports logo-related customization for selected sock styles and selected packaging materials.

Do you offer samples before bulk production?

Yes. Sampling is positioned as a key step for confirming style, quality, and branding before full production.

How long does production usually take?

Lead time depends on order volume and customization details. A more accurate schedule can be confirmed after product specifications and quantity are aligned.

Do you work with overseas buyers?

Yes. Export-oriented B2B cooperation is supported for studios, brands, wholesalers, and retailers in different markets.

Can I customize colors and grip patterns?

Yes. Product and OEM pages support custom color direction, grip pattern design, and visual positioning.

Do you support private label packaging?

Yes. Header cards, hang tags, and branded presentation directions can all be supported for private label projects.

What should I include in an inquiry?

Buyers should provide product interest, estimated quantity, target market, customization requirements, and packaging preferences.

What kind of profit margin can buyers expect?

Retail prices for Pilates grip socks are commonly around USD 15-20 in many markets. Our wholesale pricing is designed to support healthy buyer margins, but the actual margin still depends on product style, order volume, packaging, market positioning, and sales channel.

Do you support shipping and freight forwarding?

Yes. We can provide freight forwarding support if needed and help coordinate a suitable shipping solution based on your order size, destination, and delivery plan.

Still have questions?

Share your project details and the next step can be a clearer quotation, sample plan, or shipping proposal.
